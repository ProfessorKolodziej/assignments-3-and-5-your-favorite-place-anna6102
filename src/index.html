Carson Beach

Nestled along the vibrant South Boston shoreline, Carson Beach, also known as the L Street Beach, spans picturesque vistas of the Boston skyline and clear waters of the harbor. Its expansive stretch of rocky sand offers a perfect escape for sun-seekers, families, and outdoor enthusiasts alike. As part of a three-mile segment of parks along the South Boston shoreline, Carson Beach boasts ample amenities, including large shower facilities and a charming waterfront restaurant, making it a hub of activity and relaxation.

Visitors can enjoy the convenience of free entry and take leisurely strolls along the inviting boardwalk, savoring the tranquil ambiance and perhaps indulging in a delightful lunch by the water's edge. Whether you're soaking up the sun, picnicking with loved ones, or simply taking in the coastal splendor, Carson Beach promises a close getaway where the beauty of nature meets the pulse of urban life.

Travel Tips

  • Transportation

    Take the T! Traffic in Boston is rough, and the T stop at JFK/UMass is just a 10 minute walk to the beach.

    Here is the fastest route from JFK/UMass to Carson Beach.

  • Wildlife

    The seagulls can get pretty aggressive with your food! Make sure you can properly store your food with closed-lid containers so they don't steal all your food.

    This is a nice option for a seagull-safe cooler.

  • Safety

    Waterproof sandals are crucial for the rocky shoreline. Bring a beach towel and some sunscreen as well for a nice time on the sand!

    It's always a good idea to have a basic first aid kit handy for minor cuts or scrapes.

  • Furry Friends

    Dog friendly! Bring your furry friend for more fun. Just make sure to pick up after them!

    Remember that you're sharing the beach with local wildlife, so leave your area better than how you found it.
